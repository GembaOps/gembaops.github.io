Common pain points — and what clean logic does about them.

"We export it, then paste it into the other system."

Hours a week of swivel-chair data entry between tools that should just talk.A flow moves the data automatically, on a schedule or a trigger.

"Someone rebuilds the report every Monday morning."

A standing 3–4 hour task to recreate the same dashboard from fresh exports.A Power BI report refreshes itself — Monday's view is ready before Monday.

"Approvals sit in someone's inbox for days."

Work stalls because a request is buried in email and nobody knows whose turn it is.An automated approval routes it, reminds the approver, and logs the decision.

"We only find the data error after it's a problem."

A bad number rides through three systems before anyone notices.Validation rules check every record at entry and flag the bad one immediately.

"The status update is a person sending the same email all day."

Notifications that follow a fixed rule, done by hand, all shift long.A flow sends the right update to the right people automatically when the trigger hits.
