How it works

No black boxes. You see the workflow before anything gets automated.

Three steps, plain language, real numbers at the end. Here's exactly how an engagement runs.

Discovery

I go to the gemba — the floor — and map how the work actually flows today, not how the process doc says it does. We find where the hours and errors leak, and rank the opportunities by ROI.

You get: a ranked shortlist of automation opportunities with rough time/cost savings.

Design

We agree on the target before a line of anything gets built: what stays human, what gets automated, which tool fits, and the measurable result we're after. You approve the plan.

You get: a clear scope, the chosen approach (AI vs clean logic), and the success metric.

Implementation

I build it, test it against real data, document it, and prove the before/after in real numbers. Then I hand you a system that runs — and train whoever needs to own it.

You get: a working, documented automation and a measured before/after.
