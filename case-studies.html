Manual shift report → automated dashboard

Mid-size manufacturer · operations reporting

The problem

Two supervisors spent ~12 hours a week each pulling numbers from three systems into a hand-built spreadsheet, then emailing a summary. The report was always late, and the figures didn't always match.

The approach

A clean-logic workflow pulled the three sources automatically, validated them, and built a live dashboard. AI drafted the narrative summary for a supervisor to approve. No new software — Power Automate + Power BI on their existing 365.
