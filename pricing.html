How billing works

Fixed fees for projects. Hourly only when scope is genuinely open.

Project-based (standard)

A fixed fee for a fixed scope, agreed up front, typically 50% to start. You know the price before we begin — no meter running.

Retainer

A set number of hours per month for ongoing advisory and new builds. Best once you've got automations running and want to keep going.

Hourly (advisory only)

For genuinely undefined, exploratory work where a fixed scope doesn't fit yet. We move you to fixed-fee as soon as the scope is clear.
